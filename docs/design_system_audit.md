## 1. Design Direction

ReplySequence should feel:

- Fast
- Intelligent
- Confident
- Premium
- System-driven (not decorative)

The product should feel like:
> a control room for your sales follow-up pipeline

NOT:

- Overly gradient-heavy
- Indigo-washed
- Visually muddy
- Template-like AI SaaS
- Randomly styled

---

## 2. Color System (LOCKED)

### Core Roles

- **Amber (#F59E0B) — PRIMARY ACTION**
  - All primary CTAs
  - Important numbers (44%, 7x, etc.)
  - Conversion moments
  - Urgency / value

- **Teal (#06B6D4) — INTELLIGENCE**
  - AI outputs
  - Progress indicators
  - Data visualization
  - System feedback
  - Checkmarks / active flows

- **Indigo (#6366F1) — BRAND**
  - Headings accents
  - Gradient text highlights
  - Selected states
  - Navigation focus

- **Green (#22C55E) — SUCCESS**
  - Completed states
  - “Sent” badges

- **Red / Amber-Red (#EF4444) — WARNING**
  - Overdue
  - Risk flags

---

### Surfaces

- Background: `#060B18`
- Section Alt: `#0A1020`
- Card Surface: `#0F172A`

❗ Only ONE card surface allowed across site

---

### Typography Colors

- Heading: `#E8ECF4`
- Body: `#C0C8E0`
- Muted: `#8892B0`

---

## 3. Global Rules

### CTAs
- ALL primary CTAs must be **amber**
- Indigo is NOT used for primary actions
- Secondary = indigo or outline
- No mixed CTA styles

---

### Cards
- Must use ONE surface color
- Equal height within rows
- Consistent padding
- Subtle shadow + hover lift

---

### Sections
- Must feel like distinct “chapters”
- No blending dark backgrounds
- Use clear alternation

---

### Typography
- Strong hierarchy required
- No important text muted
- Headings must feel intentional

---

## 4. Component Standards

### Buttons
- Primary: amber gradient
- Secondary: indigo or outline
- Hover must be obvious

---

### Cards
- Equal height per row
- Consistent padding
- Clean structure first, styling second

---

### Pills / Badges
- Minimal usage
- No clutter in hero
- Must be readable

---

## 5. Known System Problems (Pre-Audit)

- Card misalignment across sections
- Weak layout systems
- Inconsistent CTA hierarchy
- Indigo overuse
- Teal underuse
- Product visuals too small or unclear
- Sections feel repetitive
- Weak demo visibility
