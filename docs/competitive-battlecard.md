It is built for founders, solo sellers, account managers, and SMB sales teams whose bottleneck is not taking notes or recording calls -- it is actually writing and sending the follow-up. Free tier (5 drafts/mo), Pro at $19/mo with unlimited AI drafts, Team at $29/mo with CRM sync. No annual contracts, no minimum seats, 2-minute setup.

## 3. Per-Competitor Sections

### Gong

- **What they do:** Enterprise revenue intelligence platform with deep conversation analytics, pipeline forecasting, and rep coaching.
- **Their strengths:**
  - Deep conversation analytics (talk-to-listen ratios, topic tracking, competitive mention detection)
  - Deal intelligence and pipeline forecasting
  - Enterprise-grade coaching tools for large sales orgs
- **Our advantages:**
  - We generate actual follow-up emails in 8 seconds; Gong gives you dashboards
  - $19/mo vs $100+/user/mo with no minimum seats or annual contracts
  - 2-minute setup vs weeks of enterprise onboarding
- **When to choose them over us:** You run a large sales org that needs pipeline forecasting, deal scoring, and team coaching at enterprise scale.
- **Key objection handler:** "Gong is amazing for understanding your pipeline. But after the call ends, your reps still have to write the follow-up. ReplySequence does that in 8 seconds. Many teams use both -- Gong for pipeline intelligence, ReplySequence for follow-up speed."

### Otter.ai

- **What they do:** Meeting transcription and note-taking platform with real-time collaboration and a polished mobile app.
- **Their strengths:**
  - Live transcription during meetings
  - Strong mobile app (iOS/Android)
  - Years of transcription refinement with speaker ID and custom vocabulary
- **Our advantages:**
  - We generate ready-to-send emails, not just notes you still have to act on
  - "Ask Your Meetings Anything" -- conversational AI across all meetings
  - One-click send and custom email templates
- **When to choose them over us:** You need live transcription, real-time collaboration, or heavy mobile usage (journalists, researchers).
- **Key objection handler:** "Otter gives you great notes. But notes don't close deals -- follow-up emails do. ReplySequence picks up where Otter stops. You can actually use both: Otter for live transcription, ReplySequence for the follow-up."

### Fireflies.ai

## 5. Common Objections & Responses

**"I already use Otter / Fireflies / Fathom for meeting notes."**
Those tools capture what was said. ReplySequence turns it into what you send. Notes don't close deals -- follow-up emails do. You can keep your note-taking tool and add ReplySequence for the last mile.

**"I already use Gong / Chorus for conversation intelligence."**
Different job. Gong/Chorus analyze conversations to forecast pipeline and coach reps. ReplySequence generates the actual follow-up email. Many teams use Gong for analytics and ReplySequence for follow-up speed.

**"Why do I need this if I already have meeting notes?"**
Because notes sit in a doc. Follow-up emails get sent. The gap between "I have notes" and "the client received a follow-up" is where deals stall. ReplySequence closes that gap in 8 seconds.

**"Is $19/mo worth it?"**
If you send 5+ follow-up emails per week after calls, that's roughly 2-3 hours of writing time saved. At any reasonable hourly rate, $19 pays for itself after the first email. The free tier lets you test with 5 drafts before committing.

**"We already have a CRM that logs meeting notes."**
CRM notes are for your team. Follow-up emails are for your client. ReplySequence does the second part -- generating a personalized, ready-to-send email from the actual conversation, not a generic template.

**"I can just use ChatGPT to write follow-ups."**
You can. But you have to copy the transcript, write a prompt, paste the output, edit it, format it, and send it. ReplySequence does all of that automatically in 8 seconds, connected to your meetings and CRM, with custom templates and one-click send.
