# ReplySequence Newsletter Strategy

## The Offer
**Name:** "Close the Loop" — a weekly email for sales reps who want to win more deals after the meeting ends.

**Frequency:** Weekly (Tuesdays at 8am ET — highest open rates for sales audiences)

**Platform:** Resend (already integrated) + collect via waitlist form on replysequence.com

## Value Proposition
"One actionable insight per week on closing deals faster after the call. No fluff. 2-minute read."

## Content Format
Each issue follows this structure:
1. **The Insight** (1 paragraph) — one specific sales follow-up tactic
2. **Why It Works** (2-3 sentences) — the psychology or data behind it
3. **Try This Today** (1 action item) — something they can do immediately
4. **RS Update** (optional, 1-2 sentences) — building in public update

## Lead Magnet Ideas (pick one to start)
- "The 8-Second Follow-Up Playbook" — PDF with 5 email templates that reference meeting specifics
- "Follow-Up Audit Checklist" — score your current follow-up workflow in 5 minutes
- "The Meeting-to-Deal Pipeline Map" — visual guide showing where deals leak

## Subscriber Sources
1. Waitlist signups (already collecting emails)
2. Blog readers (add inline CTA to each blog post)
3. Demo page users (after they see the AI-generated email)
4. LinkedIn content (link in posts)
5. Cold outreach replies who aren't ready to buy yet

## Metrics to Track
- Subscriber count (goal: 500 in first 3 months)
- Open rate (target: 40%+, typical for niche B2B)
- Click rate (target: 5%+)
- Reply rate (engagement signal)
- Waitlist → newsletter conversion

## Implementation Steps
1. Design a simple email template in Resend (plain text preferred — higher deliverability)
2. Add "Subscribe to Close the Loop" CTA to blog posts and /demo page
3. Write first 4 issues in advance (buffer)
4. Set up Resend audience segment for newsletter subscribers
5. Launch with existing waitlist as seed audience (ask permission first)

## NOT Doing
- No fancy HTML templates (plain text converts better for founder-led newsletters)
- No sponsorships until 1,000+ subscribers
- No daily sends (weekly is sustainable for a solo founder)
