## Channel Strategy

### Primary Channels (80% of effort)
1. **LinkedIn** - Your ICP (sales leaders) lives here
   - 3 posts/week minimum
   - Engage 30 min/day on sales content
   - DM outreach to prospects

2. **Cold Email** - Direct path to users
   - 50 personalized emails/week
   - Focus on SDR/AE managers at B2B SaaS companies
   - Lead with problem, not product

3. **Product Hunt** - One-time launch spike
   - Schedule for Tuesday
   - Line up 20+ supporters in advance
   - Prepare maker comment with story

### Secondary Channels (20% of effort)
4. **Hacker News** - Technical credibility
   - Show HN post about the tech
   - Be ready for brutal feedback
   - Best day: Tuesday-Thursday 8-10am PT

5. **Twitter/X** - Build in public
   - Daily updates on progress
   - Engage with indie hacker community
   - Share wins AND struggles

6. **Indie Hackers** - Community validation
   - Milestone posts
   - Answer questions in forums
   - Cross-promote launches

## What Made Others Successful

### Senja ($1M ARR Micro-SaaS)
- "Powered by" badges drove 30% of growth
- Affiliate program (30% commission) = 16% of signups
- Most generous free tier in category
- Focused on creators and indie founders specifically

### Linear (Premium Launch)
- Built waitlist hype before opening
- Focused on design/craft messaging
- Targeted frustrated Jira users specifically

### Plausible Analytics
- Open source + paid SaaS model
- Privacy-focused positioning vs Google Analytics
- Content marketing around "Google-free" narrative

### Key Patterns
1. **Narrow ICP** - Don't try to serve everyone
2. **Comparison pages** - Capture "X alternative" searches ✓ DONE
3. **Build in public** - Creates invested audience
4. **Ship fast, iterate** - Don't wait for perfect
5. **Personal outreach** - 1:1 beats broadcast at start
